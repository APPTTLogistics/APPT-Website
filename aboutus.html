ABOUT US

Our Journey

APP Transport & Travels, a partnership firm engaged in the field of logistic services, has been in business for over 30 years. We draw our inspiration from the rich and varied experience of our Managing Partner, Mr. A Sithivinayagam.

Our Managing Partner

Shri A. Sithivinayagam, Managing Partner of the firm, began his career in the field of Transport of goods in 1986 with a very modest beginning. He has traversed the entire length and breadth of the nation in the realms of value of business.
